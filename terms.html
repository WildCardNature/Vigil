4. Platform Fee

Vigil charges a 1% platform fee (100 basis points) on token swaps executed through the App.

  • The fee is deducted from the swap output amount.
  • The fee is collected in the output token and sent to the Vigil fee wallet.
  • The fee transfer is included in the same atomic transaction as the swap — if the swap fails, no fee is collected.
  • The fee amount is displayed in the swap interface before you confirm each trade.
  • Network fees (Solana transaction fees) are separate and paid directly to the Solana network.

We reserve the right to modify the fee rate. Any changes will be reflected in the App before you confirm a swap.
